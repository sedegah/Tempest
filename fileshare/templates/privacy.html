Privacy Policy

Effective: March 18, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service. This includes the files you upload (encrypted), your IP address for rate limiting, and temporary session data.

2. Encryption & Security

Files are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored on Cloudflare R2. We do not store your passwords in plain text; they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.

3. Data Retention

Files are automatically deleted after they expire or are downloaded (if configured for one-time use). No backups are kept after deletion.

4. Third Party Services

We use Cloudflare for storage and DDoS protection. Your data is handled according to their security protocols.
